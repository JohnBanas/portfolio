About Me

Welcome to my page!
I live and work in Nashville,TN with my wife Samantha and our puppy Teddy. I enjoy travelling both locally and globally, so be sure visit my Facebook or Instagram for pictures of some of my adventures! I believe strongly in helping others and being of service. Technology is in the unique position to give a lot of resources efficiently to the world. I want to be part of that solution.

I thouroughly enjoy the creative yet technical aspects of software development. I love to work with a team, but can be self-managed when necessary. I am a self-motivated learner who can adapt to new concepts quickly. I have front and back end software engineering skills that I am happy to put to work for you!
